Frequently Asked Questions

How flexible are the premiums?

+

You can adjust your premium payments up or down, or even skip payments if there's sufficient cash value to cover the insurance costs. This flexibility helps during financial ups and downs.

What's the difference from whole life?

+

Universal life offers flexible premiums and death benefits, while whole life has fixed premiums. Universal life typically has lower initial costs but requires more active management.

Can I change my death benefit?

+

Yes, you can increase or decrease your death benefit. Increases may require additional underwriting, while decreases can be made without medical review.
