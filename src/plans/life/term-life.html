Frequently Asked Questions

What happens when my term ends?

+

You have several options: renew the policy (usually at higher rates), convert to permanent life insurance, or let the coverage end if no longer needed.

Can I increase my coverage later?

+

Yes, you can apply for additional coverage, though it may require a new medical exam and will be based on your current age and health.

Is the death benefit taxable?

+

No, life insurance death benefits are generally income tax-free to your beneficiaries.
